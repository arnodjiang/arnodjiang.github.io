About me

Research Interests​
======
My research primarily focuses on ​multimodal large language models (MLLMs)​, with a special interest in ​alignment techniques​ (e.g., human preference alignment, cross-modal consistency). I am also passionate about developing scalable foundation models to bridge gaps between vision, language, and reasoning. Recent explorations include:

- Multimodal model training and optimization,
- MLLM alignment for robustness and controllability,
- Generative AI for cross-modal tasks.
